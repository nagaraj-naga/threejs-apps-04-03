📦 Containers

Orchestrated containerized workloads

How It Works

Containers package applications with all dependencies into lightweight, portable units.

An orchestrator (like Kubernetes/ECS) manages scheduling, scaling, and healing.

Pods group related containers. Use +/- to scale replicas and watch the orchestrator respond.
